ABOUT

We are a small non ferrous recycling facility located in Lacombe, Alberta.

We provide an extremely quick way to get your items weighed and have you paid in no time.

We are trying to play our part in a greener environment by recycling as much as we can to keep it from landfills.

SERVICES

  • Scrap Vehicle Purchase

  • Whether you bring it to us or need it picked up, we are always buying scrap vehicles at competitive pricing.

  • We are also offering pick ups in our mobile scale trailer where you can get weighed and paid at your location (non ferrous only )

FAQ

  • How much do you pay for scrap metal?

  • Scrap metal prices are based on constantly changing commodity markets. Please contact us for current market rates.

  • Do you pick up?

  • Absolutely! We love picking up. Call or message today to set up.

  • Do you have a minimum?

  • No we do not have a minimum, we accept metal in any quantity.
